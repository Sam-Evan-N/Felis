Climate Stewardship

Hydrated by the
Ancient Monsoon.

Our farms are 100% rain-fed. In a region where industrial agriculture depletes the water table, Felis Onca fibers drink only what the clouds provide.

This "water-stress" is what gives our cotton its unique strength and crisp hand-feel. It is a material birthed from the rhythm of the monsoons, not the turn of a tap.

The Philosophy

"We do not employ laborers; we partner with custodians. Our farmers and master craftswomen operate at a 15% premium above market standards, ensuring every thread is woven with dignity."

Roots in the
Regur Soil.

The Karunganni seed thrives only in the Regur—the deep, volcanic black soil of South India. This earth is rich in minerals that have nourished these specific fibers for five millennia.

By tilling this soil with bullocks rather than heavy machinery, we preserve the microbial life that gives our cotton its "living" quality. We don't just grow plants; we steward a landscape.
